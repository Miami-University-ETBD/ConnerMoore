Design FTK

Design FTK (For The Kingdom) is more than just a brand—it's a creative mission to spread faith through meaningful designs. Every piece we create is rooted in a purpose: to glorify God and inspire conversations about Him.

Our designs are crafted to be bold, expressive, and impactful, offering a fresh way for believers to wear their faith with confidence. Whether it’s a hoodie featuring scripture or a minimalist tee that sparks curiosity, each design carries a deeper message.
