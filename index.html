About Us

Design FTK (For The Kingdom) is a creative clothing brand dedicated to sharing faith through innovative and inspiring designs. We believe in using graphic design as a medium to glorify God and spread His message in a fresh and meaningful way.

Each piece is thoughtfully designed to encourage conversations about faith, provide inspiration, and support a community of believers who want to wear their faith boldly.

Our Mission

At Design FTK, we are committed to:

  • Creating high-quality, faith-inspired apparel
  • Encouraging spiritual conversations through design
  • Building a supportive community centered around faith
  • Giving back and supporting missions

Our brand is more than just clothing—it’s a movement to spread the Gospel in a creative and engaging way.
