Welcome to Design FTK

Design FTK (For The Kingdom) is a creative brand that combines graphic design with faith-driven values. Our mission is to use design as a tool to glorify God and inspire others through bold and meaningful visuals.

Founded by Conner Moore, an Emerging Technologies and Business Design student at Miami University, Design FTK was born out of a love for technology and creativity. We aim to merge innovative design with faith to create works that truly resonate.

Our Mission

At Design FTK, our goal is to:

  • Design impactful and faith-driven visuals
  • Support individuals and organizations in expressing their messages creatively
  • Expand our reach and inspire a larger community

We are committed to using creativity to make a lasting impact in the world through design.
