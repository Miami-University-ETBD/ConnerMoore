Welcome to Design FTK

At Design FTK (For The Kingdom), we believe in using creativity to inspire and make an impact. We are dedicated to creating original, thoughtful designs that not only look good but also serve a greater purpose. Our mission is to combine the power of graphic design with faith-based principles, creating visuals that glorify God and positively influence those who experience them.

Founded by Conner Moore, a passionate Emerging Technologies and Business Design (ETBD) student at Miami University, Design FTK was born out of a love for both technology and design. Conner has always been captivated by the way design shapes the world around us. Through his education and experience, he strives to merge cutting-edge technology with artistic expression to create meaningful designs that resonate deeply.

Our Goals

Our primary goal at Design FTK is to create designs that inspire others while remaining true to our values. We aim to offer high-quality graphic design services that reflect the creativity and authenticity that drives our team. As we continue to grow, we hope to reach even more individuals and organizations, helping them communicate their messages through powerful visuals.
